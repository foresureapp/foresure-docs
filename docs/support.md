## Common Questions

### "I can't connect my wallet"
*   Try refreshing the page.
*   Ensure you have the Coinbase Wallet or MetaMask extension installed.
*   Disable ad-blockers that might interfere with RPC calls.

### "My trade failed"
*   Check your **Slippage Tolerance**. If the market is volatile, increase it to 1-2%.
*   Check your **ETH Balance**. You need a tiny amount of ETH on Base for gas.
