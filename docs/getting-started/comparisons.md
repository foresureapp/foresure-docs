## Detailed Breakdown

### Foresure vs. Polymarket
*   **Liquidity Model**: Polymarket uses an Orderbook (CLOB), which is great for pros but can have wide spreads for new markets. Foresure uses a **Hybrid CAMM**, ensuring instant liquidity for every market from day one.
*   **UX**: Foresure is designed as a **Mobile-First PWA**, feeling like a native app on your phone.

### Foresure vs. Kalshi
*   **Regulation**: Kalshi is US-regulated (CFTC). This means they require KYC (Social Security Number), have strict deposit limits, and limited market types. Foresure is permissionless and global.
*   **Speed**: Kalshi deposits can take days (ACH). Foresure trades are instant.

### Foresure vs. Limitless
*   **Focus**: Limitless is extremely simplified (often just one click) due to being Parimutuel and it suffers for other types due to bounded loss. Foresure offers more advanced tools like **Dynamic Payout Charts** and **LP Positions** while keeping the UI simple.
